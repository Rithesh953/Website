Natural Wonders

Arilova, a charming district in Visakhapatnam,showcases the beautiful blend of natural wonders. The region is famous for its unique biodiversity, featuring endemic species of plants and animals that can't be found anywhere else in the world. From the iconic trees to diverse wildlife, this area offers an unforgettable experience for nature enthusiasts.

Coastal Beauty

Mudasarlova Reservoir is a significant water body located near Visakhapatnam (Vizag) in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is one of the oldest reservoirs in the region and plays a crucial role in supplying water to the city. The reservoir is surrounded by lush greenery, making it a popular spot for recreational activities and picnics.It was constructed during the British era to cater to the water supply needs of Visakhapatnam. Nestled amidst hills, the reservoir is surrounded by natural beauty, making it a serene getaway for locals and tourists.
